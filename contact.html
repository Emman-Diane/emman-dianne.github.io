Frequently Asked Questions

  • Am I allowed to bring my children?

    Unfortunately, we cannot host children at our wedding other than those who are included in the invite. We appreciate your understanding.

  • Am I allowed to bring a plus one?

    As much as we want to celebrate with everyone, the venue we fell in love with has a max capacity of 130, so we're really limited on who we can invite.

  • Have you reserved blocks of rooms at one or more hotels?

    No. But we suggest to book your hotel in Makati Poblacion area or somewhere near EDSA Mandaluyong.

  • What is the easiest way to get to and from your wedding venues?

    Grab car or taxi. Please find the directions provided in the physical and e-invitation. Click here to see maps

  • Will there be a shuttle to and/or from the venues?

    We suggest to take taxi or book a Grab Car. You can also carpool with other guests.

  • Is there a mobile bar at the party?

    Regrettably, we do not have a mobile bar on site. However, we will have a selection of liquors provided through our catering service. Additionally, we would greatly appreciate it if you could also bring along any specific liquors you'd like to enjoy. 

  • What time should I arrive at your wedding ceremony?

    Heavy traffic is common in the area, you can check on Waze or Google Maps your ETA. We highly suggest to be there 15 minutes earlier.

  • Will there be a time gap between your wedding ceremony and reception?

    Ceremony will end at around 3 pm. Registration at the reception will start at 5:00 pm and dinner will be served at 6 pm.

  • What time will your wedding reception end? Is there an after-party?

    Until around 7pm followed by the after party until 9pm, in the same venue.

  • Am I allowed to take photos at your wedding?

     Yes of course!  But not during the bridal march. We hired professionals to do it for everyone. We highly appreciate if we avoid interrupting them. There will also be a designated area for photo opportunities during the after-party. Additionally, we will furnish a webpage and a dedicated hashtag for you to share your POV with us!near EDSA Mandaluyong.

  • Where should we bring/send our wedding gift?

    Having you with us on our special day is most cherished gift we could receive! If you wish to bless us with a token, a cash gift would be greatly appreciated. Feel free to send it through the QR code provided at our webpage.

    No. But we suggest to book your hotel in Makati Poblacion area or somewhere near EDSA Mandaluyong.
