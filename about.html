God's Time

 Our Story

  In the quiet realm of digital messages, quietly their love story began. It was an early morning call for the organization's getaway. Through the glass walls of the cafe, their eyes met, and something shifted within them. Time seemed to slow down as they arrived earlier than others, and that morning held the promise of something extraordinary.
   The fellowship night became the canvas upon which their story was painted. They found themselves seated side by side, sharing laughter and stories, a bond forming amidst the clinking of cups with drawings related to the first letter of their names that also defines their personality; and of course, the crazy truths unfold by the playing cards. In that open-air revelry, they discovered the beauty of unveiling their true selves, a fun and candid way of getting to know each other's quirks.
   Since that day, their communication never ceased. Weekends were their rendezvous, their secret, hidden away from snooping eyes until Emmanuel’s birthday in 2022. The surprise on their friends' reactions was priceless, a testament to the depth of their connection.
   In just a few months of being together, a cold rainy January morning in the warmth of Emmanuel’s living room saw them agreeing on a lifetime of partnership, “Pakasal na tayo” he said while inside each other’s arms. She realized, she was never asked but she never protest and faintly agreed. It marked the beginning of their forever. In that sacred moment, amidst tears and a profound silence, they realized that the universe had been gently guiding their hearts, answering their prayers at just the right time.
   As the days turned into weeks and then months, they continued to grow, learning the intricate dance of compromise and untiring support. Challenges tested their resolve, but with each hurdle, their love emerged stronger, more resilient.
   Through it all, they held fast to the belief that God's time is perfect. They looked to the skies, finding comfort in the painted canvas of pretty peaches, and took comfort in the sight of strat-ing clouds, the rainbows after the storm. The miracles of butterflies, a reminder of God's grace and the beauty that can emerge from transformation.
   Now that they have found not just love but a true home in each other, becoming confidants and each other's greatest love. Their story is a testament to the profound power of love's embrace, a reminder that the universe watches over them faithfully, a melody that plays eternally in the chambers of their hearts.
   As they gaze upon the horizon of our tomorrows, they do so with hearts brimming with hope and souls overflowing with love. Their love story is a masterpiece, an art form crafted by two souls entwined, destined to beat as one, forever.
 
We found Each Other

Our Story

  • First Meeting – The Other Business

    December 16, 2021

     Years prior, they were among the familiar faces within the organization. On that memorable night at the MCC Christmas party in BGC, the bar pulsed with excitement. She found herself immersed in the lively 'Ice Breaker' game, carried away by the vibrant atmosphere. Amidst the animated crowd, his presence was unmistakable - a beer in hand, his gaze fixed on her. Later, in the parking lot, their paths crossed once more - she donned a cropped tee that had captured his attention, and he stood next to his lolo-looking car, a reflection of his responsible bachelor personality (hot daddy energy! lol). In that shared moment of exchanged smiles, a connection sparkled.
     It was not until the organization's annual planning session out of town in 2022, where both held officer positions, that they finally engaged in conversation. The conference room buzzed with ideas and aspirations, but amidst it all, their dialogue flowed effortlessly. They discovered shared visions, complementary strengths, and a mutual passion for their work.
     As the session progressed, so did their connection. They found themselves stealing glances, exchanging knowing smiles, their rapport deepening with each shared thought. It was in the midst of planning for the organization's future that they unknowingly began weaving their own.

  • First date – Classic People Calls for Classic Moves

    May 21, 2022

     After a consistent exchange of messages, they arranged to meet for a classic date: a movie, followed by dinner and coffee. The anticipation hung in the air, thick with the promise of shared laughter and stolen glances.
     This classic date marked the beginning of a love story that would unfold with grace and warmth. It was a night etched in their memories, a foundation upon which they built a future filled with shared adventures, quiet moments, and a love that would stand the test of time.

  • In a relationship – Kasal Agad!

    July 1, 2022

      In the early days of July, they became intimately entwined, finding a comfort in each other's presence like no other. Their love story was akin to a blossoming flower, unfurling with grace and promise. They treasured gentle moments, shared soft-spoken promises, finding solace in each other's arms. It was a whirlwind romance that propelled them towards the decision to marry sooner rather than later.
     Their love story, woven into the tapestry of the universe, serves as a testament to their enduring connection. They knew that when the time came for them to exchange vows, it would mark a celebration of the unique journey they had chosen to undertake together.
     They chose to mark their dating anniversaries with a light touch, reserving their grandest celebrations for their wedding anniversaries. From the outset of their relationship, their sights were firmly set on the path to marriage.
