God's Time

 Our Story

In the quiet realm of digital messages, quietly our love story began. It was an early morning call for our organization's getaway. Through the glass walls of the cafe, our eyes met, and something shifted within us. Time seemed to slow down as we arrived punctually, and that morning held the promise of something extraordinary.

The after-party, a gathering of friends and colleagues, became the canvas upon which our story was painted. We found ourselves seated side by side, sharing laughter and stories, a bond forming amidst the clinking of cups and the shuffle of playing cards. In that open-air revelry, we discovered the beauty of unveiling our true selves, a fun and candid way of getting to know each other's quirks.

Since that serendipitous day, our communication never ceased. Weekends were our rendezvous, our secret, hidden away from prying eyes until Emm's birthday in 2022. The surprise on our friends' faces was priceless, a testament to the depth of our connection.

In just a few months of being together, a rainy January morning in the comfort of Emm's living room saw us agreeing on a lifetime of partnership. It marked the beginning of our forever. In that sacred moment, amidst tears and a profound silence, we realized that the universe had been gently guiding our hearts, answering our prayers at just the right time.

As the days turned into weeks and then months, we continued to grow, learning the intricate dance of compromise and unwavering support. Challenges tested our resolve, but with each hurdle, our love emerged stronger, more resilient.

Through it all, we held fast to the belief that God's time is perfect. We looked to the skies, finding solace in the painted canvas of pretty peaches and blues, and took comfort in the sight of stratus clouds then the rainbows after the storm. Butterflies, those delicate miracles, a reminder of God's grace and the beauty that can emerge from transformation.

Now, with pens in our hands and hearts full of gratitude, we recount our journey. We have found not just love but a true home in each other, becoming confidants and each other's greatest love. Our story is a testament to the profound power of love's embrace, a reminder that the universe watches over us faithfully, a melody that plays eternally in the chambers of our hearts.

As we gaze upon the horizon of our tomorrows, we do so with hearts brimming with hope and souls overflowing with love. Our love story is a masterpiece, an art form crafted by two souls entwined, destined to beat as one, forever.
